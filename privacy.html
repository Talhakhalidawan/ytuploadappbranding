Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ytuploadappbranding ("we," "our," or "the App") handles your information. This application is a personal tool intended for internal use.

1. Data We Collect

The App accesses the following data through the Google YouTube Data API:

  • YouTube Channel Data: To identify the target channel for uploads.
  • Video Files: To upload content provided by the user from their local device.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data collected strictly for the purpose of functioning as a video upload tool:

  • Uploading: We use your credentials to authenticate with YouTube and upload the video files you select.
  • No External Sharing: We do not share, sell, or transfer your Google user data to any third parties. All authentication tokens are stored locally on the user's machine.

3. Data Retention

This App does not maintain an external database of your personal information. Authentication tokens (refresh tokens) are stored locally on your device and can be deleted by you at any time by clearing your local cache or revoking access via Google Security settings.
